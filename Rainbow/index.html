About Us

Rainbow Mattress is an Indian mattress brand focused on comfort and affordability. We create quality mattresses, pillow, and bedding to help you sleep better every day.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of mattresses do you offer?

We offer premium and standard mattresses in multiple sizes, designed for comfort, durability, and everyday use.

Do you provide custom size mattresses?

Yes, we manufacture mattresses based on your exact size requirements to ensure a perfect fit for your bed.

Which pillow types are available?
